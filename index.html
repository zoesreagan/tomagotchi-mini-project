HOW TO CARE FOR YOUR TAMAGOTCHI


Tamagotchi are easy to care for, if you pay close attention!

Your tamagotchi has three different levels of health: hunger, sleepiness & boredom.

Make sure these levels never reach 10, otherwise your tamagotchi will die

:(

Click the appropriate button to feed your tamagotchi, play with your tamagotchi or let your tamagotchi sleep.

Good luck!
